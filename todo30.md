Crucially, the Soul-Print was immutable, cryptographically secured, and stored on a permissioned enterprise chronicle, ensuring its veracity and verifiable authenticity, a testament to enduring intent. Lumina-powered semantic analysis tools ensured its clarity, consistency, and legal robustness, translating complex desires into actionable guidance.

The Lumina Steward was designated as the "Authorized Agent for the Custodian Conclave," possessing a distinct legal recognition and the operational clarity to manage the holdings within the covenant. This empowerment was strictly circumscribed by the Soul-Print, operating under the constant vigilance of the Veritas Core, and subject to periodic human audit by the Custodian Conclave.
