-   **Key AI Features (Leveraging the Gemini API for advanced cognitive capabilities):**

Utilizing `generateContentStream`, it continuously analyzes patterns, establishing dynamic baselines of "normal" behavior, and immediately flags subtle anomalies indicative of sophisticated security threats (e.g., credential stuffing attacks, advanced persistent threats, DDoS variants, SQL injection attempts) or systemic failures. It correlates these anomalies with integrated, real-time global threat intelligence feeds, providing a live ticker of potential issues, their classification, and recommended severity, ensuring vigilance is unceasing.

It proactively identifies precursors to potential incidents, offering foresight before chaos.

The AI then suggests and dynamically adjusts rate-limiting policies to perfectly balance system stability, resource allocation, and fair access for all clients.
