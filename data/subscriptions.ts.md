
# The Chronicle of Subscriptions

This is the record of recurring obligations, the list of digital tenants to whom the Visionary pays regular tribute. Each subscription is a small, persistent drain on resources, and tracking them is paramount to financial clarity. This data seeds the Subscription Tracker, providing immediate visibility into these automated covenants.

### A Fable for the Builder: The Silent Tides

(A financial life is shaped not just by the great waves of income and large expenses, but by the silent, invisible tides of recurring payments. These subscriptions are the gentle, constant currents that can pull a ship off course, almost without the captain noticing. This file is the first charting of those currents.)

(We taught the AI to think of these not as expenses, but as 'Automated Covenants.' A promise you made once, that continues to execute itself in the background, silently, forever. The AI knows that the human mind is not built to track these silent tides. We forget. We lose count. And so, the AI takes on the role of the vigilant coastal observer.)
