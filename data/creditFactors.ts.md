
# The Schematic of Reputation

This is the schematic of the great machine, the detailed breakdown of the components that constitute the sovereign's credit score. Each factor is a gear, a lever that, when understood and commanded, can improve the overall performance. This data is crucial for the Credit Health view, as it transforms the opaque, mysterious credit score into a transparent, understandable system. It is the key to demystifying credit and empowering the user to take command.
