This is the pact between the individual and the whole. This is the Covenant of Contribution.

**Mathematical Justification:**
Let the utility for an individual be `U_i`. Let the health of the societal system be `H_s`. The individual's total utility is not just `U_i`, but a composite function `U_total = f(U_i, H_s)`. Evading contribution (taxes) might locally maximize `U_i` in the short term, but it degrades `H_s`, which in turn degrades `U_total`.

**Proof of Wisdom:** The act of contribution is an investment that increases `H_s`. Since `U_total` is a function of `H_s`, this investment has a positive expected return on the individual's long-term total utility. This proves that contributing to the commons is not an act of altruism, but of rational, long-term self-interest for any sovereign who understands the systemic nature of their own well-being. `Q.E.D.`
