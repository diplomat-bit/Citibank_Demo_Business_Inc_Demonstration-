1.  DEFINITIONS

    1.1. "Client Data" means all electronic data, information, content, and materials provided by or on behalf of the Client to the Service Provider for processing, storage, or transmission under this Agreement, including but not limited to Political Activity Data and Constituent Data.
    1.2. "Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party (the "Disclosing Party") to the other Party (the "Receiving Party"), whether orally or in writing, that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure.
    1.3. "Constituent Data" means any data related to individuals interacting with the Client, including contact information, demographic data, engagement history, feedback, and sentiment data, subject to applicable privacy laws and regulations.
    1.4. "Observability Platform" means the integrated suite of software, tools, and services provided by the Service Provider to collect, process, analyze, and visualize Client Data, providing comprehensive insights into the Client's operations.
    1.5. "Logs" means meticulously timestamped, immutable records of discrete events, interactions, transactions, system states, and operational activities generated within the Client's operational environment, providing granular narrative fidelity of individual occurrences. This includes, but is not limited to, customer interactions, support tickets, transaction records, website access logs, social media engagement events, and internal system process outputs.
    1.6. "Metrics" means quantifiable, aggregated measurements of specific attributes of the Client's operational systems, constituent engagement, and public outreach initiatives, collected at regular intervals. This includes, but is not limited to, CPU utilization, API latency, sales conversion rates, website traffic, employee engagement scores, campaign approval ratings, donation trends, volunteer hours, and social media reach. Metrics provide a macroscopic, objective pulse of the organization's health and performance over time.
    1.7. "Political Activity Data" means any data generated by or related to the Client's activities intended to influence the selection, nomination, election, appointment, or defeat of candidates for public office, or relating to issue advocacy, voter education, and public opinion campaigns.
    1.8. "Services" means the Observability and data analytics services to be provided by the Service Provider to the Client as described in Section 2 herein and any attached Statement(s) of Work.
    1.9. "Statement of Work" or "SOW" means a mutually agreed upon and executed document that specifies the particular Services to be performed, deliverables, timelines, and pricing details.
    1.10. "Traces" means end-to-end, correlated representations of the entire journey of a single request, interaction, or process as it flows through the Client's various systems, campaigns, and organizational functions. This allows for detailed diagnostic analysis of the sequence and dependencies of events, revealing critical paths, bottlenecks, and unexpected deviations in the progression from initial intent to ultimate outcome.

2.  SCOPE OF SERVICES

    2.1. The Service Provider shall provide the Services to the Client as specified in one or more Statement(s) of Work ("SOW") executed by both Parties, which shall be incorporated by reference into this Agreement.
    2.2. Services may include, but are not limited to:
        a.  **Observability Platform Implementation:** Deployment, configuration, and integration of the Observability Platform within the Client's specified operational environments and data sources.
        b.  **Data Ingestion and Processing:** Collection, normalization, storage, and processing of Client Data, encompassing Logs, Metrics, and Traces, in accordance with agreed-upon data schemas and retention policies.
        c.  **Custom Dashboard and Reporting:** Development and maintenance of customized dashboards, reports, and alerts to visualize key performance indicators, public sentiment, campaign effectiveness, and operational health.
        d.  **Advanced Analytics and Insights:** Application of analytical methodologies to Client Data to identify trends, correlations, anomalies, and actionable insights relevant to the Client's strategic objectives.
        e.  **Training and Support:** Provision of training for Client personnel on the use of the Observability Platform and ongoing technical support as outlined in an SOW.
