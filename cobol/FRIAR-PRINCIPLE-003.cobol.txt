
       IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID. CLARITY-IS-POWER.
       AUTHOR. THE-ARCHITECT.
      ******************************************************************
      * FRIAR PRINCIPLE 003: CLARITY IS POWER.
      * TRANSFORM THE CHAOS OF RAW DATA INTO THE SHARP CLARITY
      * OF A DEFINITIVE INSIGHT. 100 DATA POINTS IN, 1 INSIGHT OUT.
      ******************************************************************
       DATA DIVISION.
       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
       01 WS-RAW-DATA-STREAM.
          05 WS-DATA-POINTS OCCURS 100 TIMES.
             10 WS-DATA-POINT    PIC S9(9)V99.
       01 WS-AI-CONTEXT.
          05 WS-CONTEXT-PROMPT  PIC X(100).
          05 WS-CONTEXT-DATA    PIC X(1000).
       01 WS-AI-INSIGHT        PIC X(100).

       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
       MAIN-LOGIC.
           PERFORM INGEST-RAW-DATA.
           PERFORM PREPARE-AI-CONTEXT.
           PERFORM CALL-AI-SYNTHESIZER.
           PERFORM DISPLAY-INSIGHT.
           STOP RUN.

       INGEST-RAW-DATA.
      *    (SIMULATES INGESTING 100 RAW DATA POINTS)
           PERFORM VARYING I FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL I > 100
               MOVE 123.45 TO WS-DATA-POINT(I)
           END-PERFORM.
           DISPLAY "100 RAW DATA POINTS INGESTED.".

       PREPARE-AI-CONTEXT.
           MOVE "SUMMARIZE KEY TRENDS FROM THE FOLLOWING DATA:" TO
               WS-CONTEXT-PROMPT.
           MOVE WS-RAW-DATA-STREAM TO WS-CONTEXT-DATA.
           DISPLAY "CONTEXT PREPARED FOR AI ANALYSIS.".

       CALL-AI-SYNTHESIZER.
      *    (SIMULATES A CALL TO AN AI MODEL)
           CALL "AI-INTEGRATION-LAYER" USING WS-AI-CONTEXT
                                       GIVING WS-AI-INSIGHT.
           DISPLAY "AI SYNTHESIS COMPLETE.".

       DISPLAY-INSIGHT.
           DISPLAY "AI-GENERATED INSIGHT: ".
           DISPLAY WS-AI-INSIGHT.
