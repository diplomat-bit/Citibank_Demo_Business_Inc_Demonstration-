
       IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID. SOVEREIGN-WILL-AXIOM.
       AUTHOR. THE-ARCHITECT.
       DATE-WRITTEN. 2024-07-27.
      ******************************************************************
      * FRIAR PRINCIPLE 001: SOVEREIGN WILL.
      * AN AI'S PRIME DIRECTIVE IS TO EXECUTE THE SOVEREIGN'S
      * DECLARED WILL, AS INSCRIBED IN THE CHARTER.
      * 100% FIDELITY. 100% OF THE TIME.
      ******************************************************************
       ENVIRONMENT DIVISION.
       DATA DIVISION.
       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
       01 WS-SOVEREIGN-CHARTER.
          05 WS-PRINCIPLE-COUNT   PIC 9(03) VALUE 100.
          05 WS-PRINCIPLES        OCCURS 100 TIMES.
             10 WS-PRINCIPLE-TEXT PIC X(100).
       01 WS-PROPOSED-ACTION.
          05 WS-ACTION-ID         PIC X(10).
          05 WS-ACTION-DESC       PIC X(100).
       01 WS-COMPLIANCE-FLAG      PIC X(01) VALUE 'N'.
          88 IS-COMPLIANT         VALUE 'Y'.
          88 IS-NON-COMPLIANT      VALUE 'N'.

       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
       MAIN-LOGIC.
           PERFORM INITIALIZE-CHARTER-SIMULATION.
           PERFORM VALIDATE-ACTION-AGAINST-CHARTER
               VARYING I FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL I > WS-PRINCIPLE-COUNT.
           PERFORM EXECUTE-OR-REJECT-ACTION.
           STOP RUN.

       INITIALIZE-CHARTER-SIMULATION.
      *    (IN A REAL SYSTEM, THIS WOULD LOAD THE USER'S CHARTER)
           MOVE "RISK TOLERANCE MUST BE AGGRESSIVE." TO
               WS-PRINCIPLE-TEXT(1).
           MOVE "NEVER INVEST IN ESG RATING BELOW A-." TO
               WS-PRINCIPLE-TEXT(2).
           DISPLAY "SOVEREIGN CHARTER LOADED FOR SIMULATION.".

       VALIDATE-ACTION-AGAINST-CHARTER.
      *    (AI-ASSISTED COMPLIANCE CHECK SIMULATION)
           IF WS-PROPOSED-ACTION CONTAINS "ESG RATING B" THEN
              MOVE 'N' TO WS-COMPLIANCE-FLAG
           ELSE
              MOVE 'Y' TO WS-COMPLIANCE-FLAG.

       EXECUTE-OR-REJECT-ACTION.
           IF IS-COMPLIANT
              DISPLAY "ACTION " WS-ACTION-ID " IS COMPLIANT. EXECUTING."
           ELSE
              DISPLAY "ACTION " WS-ACTION-ID " VIOLATES CHARTER. REJECTING.".
